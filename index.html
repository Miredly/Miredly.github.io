@Miredly

I'm a game and interactive media developer, currently looking to join a small team.

I got my masters in Music Technology at Berklee College of Music, where I worked on a VR audio installation piece that was shown at the Museum of Arts & Sciences in Valencia, Spain.

I've been lucky enough to work with incredibly talented people at Harvard, the Smithsonian, and NASA, on interactive media with the mission of making the world a kinder, better, and more scientifically literate place.

I want to live a quiet life, and make beautiful things with like-minded friends.
